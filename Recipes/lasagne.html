Simple Spinach Lasagna

This Lasagne recipe is so yummy, and is easy to make


Description

This recipe is to make a vegetarian Lasagne.
This Recipe is tailor made specifically for vegetarians and
is able to accomodate them without forcing them to eat soya.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
  • ½ medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 2 cups non-fat cottage cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package part skim mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ½ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 uncooked lasagna noodles
