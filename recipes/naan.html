How to make Naan


The ingredients

  1. 1 Package active dry yeast
  2. 1 cup warm water
  3. 1/4 cup white sugar
  4. 3 tablespoons milk
  5. 1 large egg, beaten
  6. 2 teaspoons salt
  7. 4 1/2 cups of bread flour
  8. 1/4 cup butter, melted
  9. optional addon: 2 teaspoons minced garlic (garlic butter naan)

Directions for cooking

Step 1

In a large bowl, add warm water, dissolve the yeast ub warm water and let the bowl stand for about 10 mins until it is frothy

Step 2

While waiting, genrously oil a large bowl

Step 3

Stir sugar, milk, egg and salth into the yeast mixture. Mix enough flour to make a soft dough.

Step 4

Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth, for 6 mins

Step 5

Place dough in prepared oil, cover with damp cloth and let rise for about 1 hour (*yeast rises best in warm areas:))

Step 6

Once risen, punch down dough on a lightly floured surface than knead in garlic. Then make snall handfuls of dough approx. the size of golf balls.
Cover with towel and let rise until doubled in size for about 30 mins

Step 7

Preheat a grill pan on high heat
Then roll each dough ball into a thin circle
PLace into pan and cook until lightly brown and fluffy for about 2 -3 mins
flip and cook the other side for about 2 - 4 more minutes and repeat for the rest of the Naan
